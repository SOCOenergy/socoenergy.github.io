About

SOCO Industrial is a mobile hydraulic service and repair business operating in southern Colorado. We are available 24/7 and will typically arrive on-site 1-hour after a service call is initiated. Our fully loaded service trucks enable us to conduct a range of hydraulic troubleshooting and repair. We can make custom hoses on-site and we can stock cylinders, pumps, motors, valves or anything else you need specific to your equipment. Call or text (719) 877-5139

Our service area is generally an hour radius around Cañon City; north to Hartsle, south to Westcliffe, east to Pueblo, and west to Salida. Service calls within the Cañon City/Florence city limits are free. Beyond the city limits and up to 25 miles is $50 to dispatch a service truck & technician. 25 miles or more is $100 to dispatch a service truck & technician.
